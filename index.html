Why nRev?

Every healthy RevOps organization has five jobs to keep the rocket in the air

  • Plan & Forecast – Decide where the revenue will come from and who owns which number.
  • Manage Data – Keep every lead, deal, and dollar clean and visible in the CRM.
  • Own Systems – Make the tech stack (Salesforce, Marketo, etc.) work in sync.
  • Orchestrate Processes – Ensure money moves smoothly from lead to cash (and back for renewals).
  • Enable the Team – Give Sales, Marketing, and CS the playbooks and training that win deals.

The Problem: Myriads of Opinionated SaaS Tools working in Silos

Gong logs calls, Clari forecasts pipeline, Chili Piper books meetings, Rattle nudges reps—each brilliant at one thing yet blind to the rest. Because SaaS tools are opinionated and siloed, RevOps spends its days stitching CSVs, writing brittle integrations, and troubleshooting workflows instead of steering revenue strategy.

The Vision: A Canvas of AI Agents

Consider a system that unified the revenue stack to a single canvas of agents that could talk to first‑party data (data lying in CRMs, calls, emails) as well as access hundreds of third‑party vendors (e.g., people graphs, firmographics, job‑change signals, custom research agents, and hundreds more).

It is a platform that will allow RevOps to access and use LLM and MCP agents and talk to all systems as required and to autonomously handle processes and tasks.
